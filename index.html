Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

ArrStack does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your server connection details (such as URLs and API keys) are stored exclusively on your device and, if enabled, in your personal iCloud account. Your data never reaches us or any third party.

Data Collection: None. ArrStack does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Network Requests: The app connects only to servers you configure. No data is sent to ArrStack or any third party.

iCloud Sync: If enabled, your server configurations are synced through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services: ArrStack integrates with services you run on your own infrastructure (Sonarr, Radarr, etc.). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Purchases: All billing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.
