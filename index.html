ArrStack Privacy Policy

Privacy for ArrStack
on iOS and Android

ArrStack is a client app for connecting to the media and download services you choose to run. ArrStack does not operate a backend that receives your server activity, media library data, or connection details.

No analytics
No ads

What ArrStack Does Not Collect

ArrStack does not collect, receive, or sell personal data through ArrStack-operated servers.

  • No analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No crash-reporting or telemetry SDKs operated by ArrStack
  • No media library contents sent to ArrStack
  • No server connection details uploaded to ArrStack

What the App Stores to Work

ArrStack stores information locally so it can reconnect to the services you configure.

  • Server names, URLs, ports, and optional base paths
  • API keys, usernames, passwords, custom headers, and similar connection credentials
  • Session data such as authentication cookies when needed for compatible services
  • App settings, preferences, and feature state

Network Requests

The app connects directly from your device to the servers and services you configure, such as Sonarr, Radarr, Overseerr, SABnzbd, NZBGet, qBittorrent, Bazarr, Tautulli, and Uptime Kuma. ArrStack does not proxy these requests through ArrStack servers.

If you contact support by email, your email provider handles that message. If you make a purchase, Apple or Google handles billing on its platform.

Android

On Android, ArrStack stores your configuration locally on your device. Sensitive values are protected locally on-device.

ArrStack excludes its main configuration store and session-cookie store from Android cloud backup and device-transfer backup rules.

If you manually export a backup file from inside the app, that exported file is under your control and may contain your service configuration data.

iOS

On iOS, ArrStack stores your configuration on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your configuration may sync through your personal iCloud account using Apple services such as CloudKit.

Apple handles that synced data under Apple's own terms and privacy practices. If iCloud sync is disabled, your configuration remains local to your device unless you export it yourself.

Purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple on iOS and by Google on Android. ArrStack does not receive your full payment card details or full payment credentials.

Retention and Deletion

ArrStack does not keep a server-side copy of your app data. Your data stays on your device, in your own Apple iCloud account if you enable iCloud sync on iOS, or in any manual backup files you create.

You can reduce or remove stored data by:

  • Deleting servers or clearing app data on your device
  • Uninstalling the app
  • Deleting any manual backup files you exported
  • Turning off iCloud sync on iOS and removing synced data using your Apple account tools

Third-Party Services

ArrStack connects to services that you operate or choose to use. Their privacy, security, and data handling practices are controlled by those services and their operators, not by ArrStack.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version is indicated by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact
